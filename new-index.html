Our Story

Bringing Bihar's culinary heritage to your plate

NS Food Paradise was born from a passion to showcase the rich culinary heritage of Bihar to the world. Founded by Nishikant, a second-year student from Bihar with a deep love for his homeland's cuisine, our restaurant aims to preserve traditional recipes while presenting them in a contemporary setting.

Every dish at NS Food Paradise tells a story of Bihar's diverse cultural influences, from the Mughal era to the present day. Our chefs, trained in authentic Bihari cooking techniques, use locally-sourced ingredients to create dishes that are true to their origins.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Ravi Kumar brings over 15 years of experience in traditional Bihari cuisine. Born and raised in Patna, he learned the art of cooking from his grandmother, who passed down recipes that had been in their family for generations. His innovative approach to traditional dishes has earned him recognition in culinary circles across India.
